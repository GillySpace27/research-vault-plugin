# Proposal Ideas

Idea-first capture, before an idea is matched to a specific solicitation. Once
an idea is matched to a call, promote it to `proposal-solicitations.md` (and
usually its own project file).

## Status states

`Raw` → `Sketched` → `Scoping solicitation` → `Promoted`

(`Scoping solicitation` and `Promoted` map to the "Proposal Idea Scoped" bucket
in `/status`.)
